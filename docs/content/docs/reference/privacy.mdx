# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

## Overview

Tuish is a licensing and monetization platform for terminal applications. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## What We Collect

### For Developers (Platform Users)

- **Account Information**: Email address and Stripe Connect account details for payment processing
- **Product Data**: Information about your CLI products, pricing, and license configurations
- **Usage Analytics**: Aggregate API usage statistics

### For End Users (License Holders)

- **License Keys**: Cryptographic license keys stored locally on your machine
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect personal information from end users. License verification is designed to work offline.

## How We Use Data

- Process payments through Stripe
- Generate and verify license keys
- Provide customer support
- Improve our services

## Data Storage

- Account data is stored securely on Cloudflare infrastructure
- License keys are stored locally on end users' machines
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Offline-First Design

Tuish is designed with privacy in mind:

- License verification works offline using Ed25519 cryptographic signatures
- No network calls are required after initial license activation
- End user data never leaves their machine

## Your Rights

You may request to:

- Access your data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data

## Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@tuish.dev

## Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email.
